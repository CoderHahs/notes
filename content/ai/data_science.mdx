The main tasks of data science are to **store** and **explore**.

Data Engineering:

- **Extract** data from multiple internal and external sources
- **Analyse** data to determine and improve the quality
- **Preprocess** data to discard irrelevant information

Data Exploration and _Mining_:

- **Employ** analytics programs, machine learning and statistical methods to build models
- **Explore and examine** data from a variety of angles to determine weaknesses, trends, etc.

Content in this section focuses on:

- Database programing
- Data preprocessing skills
- Data mining skills
- Data visualization
